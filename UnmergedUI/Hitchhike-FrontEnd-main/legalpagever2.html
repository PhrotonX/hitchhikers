Safety Guidelines

Your safety is important to us. Follow these guidelines to protect yourself while using our services.

1. Account Security

Protect your account with these practices:

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available
  • Never share your login credentials
  • Log out from shared devices

2. Personal Information

Be cautious about what information you share:

  • Limit sharing of personal contact information
  • Be wary of requests for financial information
  • Think carefully before sharing location data
  • Report suspicious requests to our support team

3. Meeting Others

If our service involves meeting others:

  • Meet in public, well-lit areas
  • Tell a friend or family member about your plans
  • Arrange your own transportation
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave

4. Reporting Concerns

Report any safety concerns immediately:

  • Use our in-app reporting features
  • Contact our support team with details
  • In case of emergency, contact local authorities
